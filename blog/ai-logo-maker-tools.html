Best AI Logo Maker Tools in 2026

📅 July 24, 2026 ⏱ 8 min read 👤 AIToolsNova Team 🏷️ AI Tools

A professional logo is essential for any brand. In 2026, AI-powered logo makers make it possible to create stunning logos in minutes, without hiring a designer. Whether you're starting a business, launching a blog, or building a personal brand, there's an AI logo maker for you.

💡 Quick Note: Most AI logo makers offer free trials or free downloads with watermarks. Premium plans unlock high-resolution downloads.

Top AI Logo Maker Tools in 2026

1. Looka

What it does: Looka uses AI to create logos based on your preferences. You choose your industry, colors, and style, and Looka generates dozens of options.

Pricing: Free (low-res) and Paid plans starting at $20

2. Canva Logo Maker

What it does: Canva's AI logo maker generates logo options based on your inputs. It's easy to use and integrated with Canva's design platform.

Pricing: Free (basic) and Pro ($12.99/month)

3. Brandmark

What it does: Brandmark creates logos, color schemes, and branding packages with AI. Great for startups and small businesses.

Pricing: Paid plans starting at $25

4. Hatchful (by Shopify)

What it does: Hatchful is Shopify's free logo maker that creates logos based on your industry and style preferences.

Pricing: Completely free

5. LogoAI

What it does: LogoAI uses AI to generate logos and brand identity packages. It offers extensive customization options.

Pricing: Paid plans starting at $29

6. Designs.ai Logo Maker

What it does: Designs.ai offers a comprehensive logo maker with AI-powered design suggestions and full branding packages.

Pricing: Paid plans starting at $29/month

7. Tailor Brands

What it does: Tailor Brands creates logos and full branding packages, including social media assets and business cards.

Pricing: Paid plans starting at $3.99/month

How to Choose the Right AI Logo Maker

  • For Free Logos: Hatchful or Canva
  • For Professional Logos: Looka or Brandmark
  • For Branding Packages: Tailor Brands or LogoAI
  • For Beginners: Canva Logo Maker

Tips for Creating Great AI Logos

  • Be Specific: Choose your industry, colors, and style carefully.
  • Keep It Simple: Simple logos are more memorable and versatile.
  • Test Multiple Options: Generate several logos and compare them.
  • Get Feedback: Ask others for opinions before finalizing.
  • Download High-Res: Ensure you have high-resolution files for all uses.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Which AI logo maker is best for free?

Hatchful and Canva Logo Maker offer completely free options with good quality.

Q2: Can I use AI-generated logos for my business?

Yes, most AI logo makers allow commercial use. Check each tool's terms of service.

Q3: How long does it take to create a logo?

Most AI logo makers generate options in minutes.

Q4: Do I own the rights to my AI-generated logo?

Yes, when you purchase a logo, you typically own the rights. Free versions may have limitations.
