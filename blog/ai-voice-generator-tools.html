Top AI Voice Generator Tools in 2026

1. ElevenLabs

What it does: Create realistic, human-like voiceovers with emotional expression.

Pricing: Free (limited) and Paid plans starting at $5/month

2. Murf

What it does: AI voiceover tool with multiple voices and languages.

Pricing: Free (limited) and Paid plans starting at $19/month

3. Play.ht

What it does: Text-to-speech tool with realistic voices and API access.

Pricing: Free (limited) and Paid plans starting at $14.25/month

4. Respeecher

What it does: Professional voice cloning for content creators and studios.

Pricing: Paid plans (custom pricing)

5. Voice.ai

What it does: AI voice changer and voiceover tool with real-time processing.

Pricing: Free (limited) and Paid plans

6. Descript AI

What it does: AI voiceover and audio editing tool with transcription.

Pricing: Free (limited) and Paid ($24/month)
