Best AI Website Builder Tools in 2026

📅 July 24, 2026 ⏱ 8 min read 👤 AIToolsNova Team 🏷️ AI Tools

Building a website in 2026 is easier than ever with AI website builders. These tools allow anyone to create professional, stunning websites without coding or design skills.

💡 Quick Note: Perfect for small businesses, personal brands, and beginners.

Top AI Website Builder Tools in 2026

1. Wix AI Website Builder

What it does: Wix AI asks questions about your needs and creates a custom website in minutes.

Pricing: Free (with ads) and Paid plans starting at $17/month

2. Squarespace AI

What it does: Squarespace uses AI to create beautiful, professional websites with powerful design templates.

Pricing: Paid plans starting at $16/month

3. Hostinger AI Builder

What it does: Hostinger's AI builder creates a website in minutes with simple questions.

Pricing: Paid plans starting at $2.99/month

4. Durable AI

What it does: Durable generates a complete website with copy, images, and design in seconds.

Pricing: Free (limited) and Paid plans

5. 10Web AI

What it does: 10Web uses AI to build WordPress websites quickly and efficiently.

Pricing: Paid plans starting at $10/month

6. Jimdo AI

What it does: Jimdo AI asks questions and builds a custom website based on your answers.

Pricing: Free (limited) and Paid plans

7. Bookmark AI

What it does: Bookmark uses AI to create websites with the "AIDA" system — fast and easy.

Pricing: Free (limited) and Paid plans starting at $11.99/month

How to Choose the Right AI Website Builder

  • For Beginners: Wix AI or Jimdo AI
  • For Professional Design: Squarespace AI
  • For Budget: Hostinger AI Builder
  • For Speed: Durable AI

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Which AI website builder is best?

Wix AI and Squarespace AI are excellent, user-friendly options.

Q2: Are there free AI website builders?

Yes, Wix (with ads), Jimdo, and Durable offer free versions.

Q3: Can I customize my AI-built website?

Yes, all builders allow customization after the AI creates the initial design.

Q4: How long does it take to build a website?

AI builders can create a website in 5-10 minutes.
