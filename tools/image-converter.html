What is Image Converter?

Image Converter is a free online tool that lets you convert images between JPG, PNG, and WebP formats instantly. Whether you need to convert a PNG to JPG for web use, or a JPG to WebP for better compression, our Image Converter makes it quick and easy.

💡 Key Feature: Convert between JPG, PNG, and WebP formats with a single click — completely free and no signup required.

How to Use Image Converter

  1. Upload Image: Click the upload area or drag and drop your image.
  2. Select Format: Choose your desired output format (JPG, PNG, or WebP).
  3. Convert: Click "Convert & Download" to process your image.
  4. Download: Your converted image will be downloaded automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Is Image Converter free?

Yes, Image Converter is completely free with no limits.

Q2: What formats are supported?

JPG, PNG, and WebP formats are supported.

Q3: Will my image quality be affected?

Image quality is preserved during conversion. WebP may have slightly smaller file sizes.

Q4: Can I convert transparent images?

PNG supports transparency. When converting to JPG, transparency will be replaced with a white background.
