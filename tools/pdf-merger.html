What is PDF Merger?

PDF Merger is a free online tool that lets you combine multiple PDF files into a single document. Whether you need to merge invoices, reports, or any other PDF files, our PDF Merger makes it quick and easy.

💡 Key Feature: Merge unlimited PDF files into one document in seconds — completely free and no signup required.

How to Use PDF Merger

  1. Upload PDFs: Click the upload area or drag and drop your PDF files.
  2. Review Files: See the list of uploaded files in the file list.
  3. Merge: Click "Merge & Download" to combine all PDFs.
  4. Download: Your merged PDF will be ready for download.

Benefits of Using PDF Merger

  • Organize Documents: Combine related PDFs into one file.
  • Save Time: Merge documents in seconds.
  • Professional Look: Create professional, unified documents.
  • 100% Free: No limits, no signup, no hidden charges.

Example Use Cases

  • Invoices: Merge multiple invoices into a single PDF.
  • Reports: Combine report chapters into one document.
  • Contracts: Merge signed contracts into one file.
  • Presentations: Combine slides into a single PDF.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Is PDF Merger free?

Yes, PDF Merger is completely free with no limits.

Q2: How many files can I merge?

You can merge unlimited PDF files at once.

Q3: Are my files stored on your servers?

No, your files are processed securely and are not stored permanently.

Q4: What happens to the file order?

Files are merged in the order they are uploaded.
